Mini Motorways

My guide to achieving excellent runs in Mini Motorways

Tips and Tricks

Create shortest distance between stores and homes

Rationale

  • Saves on extra road tiles
  • Quick car movements
  • Less likely to hit congestion
  • Manages unhandled orders at the store

Avoid the traffic lights (Current Patch)

Rationale

  • Actually slows down traffic
  • Increases congestion
  • Traffic lights react slowly
  • Missed opportunities for roundabouts, roadways, bridges, etc

Motorways are your saviors

Rationale

  • Extremely versitile
  • Substitute long distance roadways
  • Avoids traffic
  • Cars move faster
  • Direct path to anywhere

Hit the pause

Rationale

  • Time to rethink your approach
  • Prevents or stops congestion
  • Allows time to fix mistakes
